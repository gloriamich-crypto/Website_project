Different flower arrangements

Bouquets

This is the most common flower arrangement, where the stems are tied together and wrapped in paper.

Triangular

Taller stems are placed first to make the frame which creates a striking triangle shape.

Torch

Consists of tall, vertical stems arranged in a circular pattern.

Crescent

Created by arranging flowers in a curved, crescent shape.

L-Shaped

Created by arranging flowers in an L-shape, often used for table arrangements.
